## Technical Implementation

### Core Libraries Integration
1. **Anime.js**: Micro-interactions, smooth transitions, loading animations
2. **Splitting.js**: Advanced text effects for headings and CTAs
3. **Matter.js**: Physics-based product interactions and cart animations
4. **p5.js**: Creative background effects and data visualizations
5. **ECharts.js**: User dashboard charts and analytics
6. **Pixi.js**: High-performance visual effects and shaders
7. **Typed.js**: Typewriter effects for dynamic content
8. **Splide.js**: Product carousels and image galleries
9. **Shader-park**: Advanced background shaders and effects

### Responsive Design Strategy
- **Mobile-first approach** with progressive enhancement
- **Flexible grid system** adapting to all screen sizes
- **Touch-optimized interactions** for mobile devices
- **Performance optimization** for smooth 60fps animations

### Data Management
- **Mock product database** with realistic futuristic products
- **Local storage** for cart persistence and user preferences
- **Simulated AI responses** for search and recommendations
- **Real-time updates** without page refreshes
